- Cloud platform: Kaggle

- CPU/RAM: Kaggle notebook default CPU/RAM; exact CPU model was not recorded

Training time:
- Approximate time per epoch: 5 minutes, including validation
- Main final training run: 32 full epochs plus a partial 33rd epoch
- Best checkpoint selected at epoch 28 based on validation IoU
- Approximate final-run wall-clock training time: 2.7 hours
- Additional evaluation/threshold sweep time: approximately 20 to 30 minutes

Constraints:
- The final approach was shaped by severe class imbalance, cross-scene domain shift, limited training time, and the need to keep the code reproducible.
- No external remote-sensing datasets or additional remote-sensing imagery were used for training or fine-tuning.
- Training was moved from the local RTX 3050 Laptop GPU to Kaggle T4 GPUs because the local 4 GB GPU was slow and thermally constrained.
